You’ve heard stories about an abandoned warehouse at the waterfront in your town. It’s narrow structure towers over nearby buildings, nearly all the windows broken and walls eroded from years and years of exposure to the ocean’s salty air. You are tempted to approach, but hesitate when you see a “no trespassing” sign.

As you approach the side door of the building, you see a truck pull up to the side door. Someone exits the car, but you can't get a good look from far away. Maybe it's a man, maybe a woman? You can't tell. They walk briskly inside.

You open the garage door

You suddenly feel the ground rumble. Adrenaline kicks in. What could that be? The shaking suddenly stops, and you hear a loud sound in the other room.

You look around the room and see a stairwell.

Ooo... there's something shiny on the floor.
