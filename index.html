You’ve heard stories about an abandoned warehouse at the waterfront in your town. It’s narrow structure towers over nearby buildings, nearly all the windows broken and walls eroded from years and years of exposure to the ocean’s salty air. You are tempted to approach and see a “no trespassing” sign.

As you approach the side door of the building, you see a truck pull up to the side door.

Suddenly, someone comes up behind you and grabs you.
