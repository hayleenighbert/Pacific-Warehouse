You’ve heard stories about an abandoned warehouse at the waterfront in your town. It’s narrow structure towers over nearby buildings, nearly all the windows broken and walls eroded from years and years of exposure to the ocean’s salty air. You are tempted to approach, but hesitate when you see a “no trespassing” sign.

As you approach the side door of the building, you see a truck pull up to the side door.

Suddenly, someone comes up behind you and grabs you.

You suddenly feel the ground rumble. Adrenaline kicks in. What could that be? The lights above start swinging back and forth and the floor shakes, scattering broken glass and objects across the floor. The shaking suddenly stops, and you hear a loud sound in the other room.
